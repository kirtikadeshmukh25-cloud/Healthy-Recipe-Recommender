Recipe of Cake

This is the list of ingredients. Please try this at home.

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 3/4 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°C. Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the dry ingredients.
  3. Add milk, eggs, oil, and vanilla. Mix until smooth.
  4. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin, which is normal).
  5. Pour batter into prepared pans and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Cool for 10 minutes, then remove from pans and frost.

Simple Chocolate Frosting (Optional)

  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 3 cups powdered sugar

Pizza

How to make pizza? Here is the recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza base (ready-made or homemade)
  • 3–4 tbsp pizza sauce
  • Grated mozzarella cheese (as needed)
  • Vegetables (choose your favorites):
    • Capsicum
    • Onion
    • Tomato
    • Sweet corn
    • Mushroom (optional)
  • Oregano, chili flakes, and salt (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or butter

How to Make Pizza

  1. Preheat the oven to 180–200°C (350–400°F).
  2. Place your prepared pizza on a baking tray and bake for 15–20 minutes.
  3. Alternatively, cook on a stovetop for 10-15 minutes, checking often to avoid burning.
